Start Your Engines: Welcome to the Team

Artificial Intelligence isn't a robot that washes cars. It's a Digital Shop Hand that has read every manual, forum, and chemistry book in existence.

The Golden Rule: "Treat it like a Trainee"

If you tell a new guy "Go clean that car," he might just spray it with water.
If you say, "Perform a two-bucket wash on that Porsche and dry with the twisted-loop towel," he’ll do it right.

AI is the same. You must be specific.

  • ❌ Bad: "Write an email."
  • ✅ Good: "Write a polite email to Mrs. Jones telling her the SUV is ready, but remind her not to get it wet for 24 hours because the sealant is curing."

⚠️ Safety Disclaimer

AI is smart, but it doesn't have eyes. Always check the labels on your chemicals. If the AI suggests mixing two chemicals, double-check that it is safe. Never mix bleach and ammonia based on AI advice.
