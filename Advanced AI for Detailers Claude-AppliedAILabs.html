AI Basics: Your First Lap

What is AI, Really?

AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Google Gemini are like having an extremely well-read assistant. They've read almost everything on the internet, but they need YOU to tell them what to do.

Getting Started (3 Easy Steps)

  1. Pick Your Tool: Go to chat.openai.com (ChatGPT) or claude.ai - both free!
  2. Sign Up: Just need an email, takes 30 seconds
  3. Start Typing: Ask it anything!

The CCC Method (Your Secret Formula)

To get great results every time, use the CCC Method:

  1. Context: Who are you? ("I'm a detailing service advisor...")
  2. Content: What do you need? ("Write a text message...")
  3. Constraints: How should it look? ("Keep it under 160 characters, friendly tone")

Bad vs. Good Prompts

‚ùå Bad Prompt

Back of Shop: Efficiency Engine

1. The Product Researcher

Scenario: Weird Stain on Alcantara

"I'm detailing a car with Alcantara seats. There's a coffee stain. What's the safest process to clean this without damaging the fibers? Give me a step-by-step list with product recommendations."

Note: AI is smart, but always test a small spot first!

2. Checklist Creation

Build Consistency

"Create a quality control checklist for a 'Level 2 Interior Detail.' Include steps for vacuuming, steam cleaning vents, leather conditioning, and glass cleaning. Put it in logical order starting from the driver's side. Make it printable."

Result: Professional checklist you can laminate and use daily!

3. Training New Staff

"Create a 5-day training plan for a new detailing technician with zero experience. Each day should include skills to learn, a demo task, supervised practice, and a checklist. Emphasize safety and efficiency."

4. Inventory Math Helper

Hate Math? AI's Got You

"We use about 2 ounces of ceramic coating per car. We have 3 bottles left, and each bottle is 50ml. How many cars can we coat before we run out? (Note: 1 ounce is roughly 30ml). Include a safety buffer recommendation."

5. SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) Builder

"Create an SOP for a full exterior detail on a mid-size SUV. Include: prep wash, clay bar, polish, wax, tire dressing, and final inspection. Add time estimates for each step and common mistakes to avoid."
‚ö†Ô∏è Safety First: Always verify chemical recommendations with your shop's approved products. AI is helpful but doesn't know your specific inventory!

Quick Tips for Better Results

  • Start with "Act as a..." to set the role
  • Be specific about tone (professional, friendly, casual)
  • Include length limits when needed
  • Ask for multiple options to choose from
  • Tell AI to "make it shorter" or "make it more professional" if needed
  • Always review before sending - you're the quality control!
