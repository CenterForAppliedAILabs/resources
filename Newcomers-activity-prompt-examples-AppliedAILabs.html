How to Use These Examples

These examples can serve as ready-to-use prompts for your own activities, but they also offer a broader lesson in how different large language models respond to the exact same request. Some models produce more sophisticated, detailed outputs while others keep things simple and direct. Most provide at least a solid foundation you can build on or adapt to your needs.

Pay attention to the interface as well — the way prompts are displayed, formatted, and structured varies from model to model. That presentation is shaped by each model's training and the wrappers its platform puts around the output. Comparing these side-by-side is a practical way to understand which model's style and depth best fits your workflow.
