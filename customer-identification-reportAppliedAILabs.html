07 · Strategic Recommendations

Five moves to make this work

Beyond the tool itself, these organizational moves determine whether the system actually generates revenue.

1 · Start Small, Learn Fast

Don't overhaul the CRM. Begin with HubSpot free + LinkedIn Sales Navigator + ChatGPT (~$85/mo). Upgrade only when you hit a growth target like "50+ active prospects."

2 · Designate an AI Champion

One person owns AI tool adoption — experiments daily, runs weekly 10-minute team huddles, and builds an internal library of "prompts that work for our industry."

3 · Focus Emerging Industries First

Medical is core, but aerospace, biotech, and EV offer 40%+ higher margins, faster growth, and less competition. Build targeted campaigns there first.

4 · Build Content Moats

Create industry-specific technical content (femtosecond vs. fiber, nitinol biocompatibility). It becomes a sales-enablement asset reps share to build credibility without heavy selling.

5 · Measure Everything

Set baselines now (sales cycle length, cost per qualified lead, win rate, pipeline value). Target after 90 days: 20–30% speed-to-lead improvement, 15–25% more qualified pipeline, 10–20% better win rates. Track weekly: leads by source, lead quality, time-to-first-response, email opens, pipeline velocity.

Appendix C · Compliance guardrails

Terms of Service

Respect robots.txt, adhere to rate limits (~1 req/sec), never bypass paywalls. Use official APIs; cache to minimize redundant calls.

Data Privacy

GDPR/CCPA: focus on company-level data. Don't store personal data without consent. Implement 2-year retention policies.

Anti-Spam (CAN-SPAM)

Every email: unsubscribe link, physical address, accurate sender. Honor opt-outs within 10 business days.

Audit Trail

Log all data sources & collection dates. Document transformation logic. Version-control all scraping scripts.
