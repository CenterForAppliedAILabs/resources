Coach Summary

Primary pattern and highest-value opportunities.

Primary Pattern Identified Good sequencing and decent speed, but a pattern of left-biased pressure, steep hand path, and variable vertical timing.
Power Profile Speed is currently more hands-driven than rotation-driven, with modest X-Factor Stretch.

Recommended Priorities

1. Shallow hand path + reduce leftward crash Improve path and pressure first.
2. Tempo standardization Live in the 3.3–3.5 range.
3. Vertical timing Earlier, more consistent push with better posture retention.
4. Add rotational stretch Small increase in downswing separation for more efficient speed.

Prescribed Drills

Keep practice focused and constraint-based.

Drill 1: Feet-Together Shallow Path Hit half-wedges with feet together, feeling the club swing more around you, not down and left.
Drill 2: Tempo 3-Count Count “1-2-3” to the top, “4” to impact; record swings and match your best-contact tempos.
Drill 3: Vertical Timing Pump Rehearse 3 pumps where you stay in posture, then push up late (near impact), not early.
