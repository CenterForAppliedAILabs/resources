Week 1–2: Rhythm Retraining

Drill #1: Metronome Tempo Mapping PRIORITY #1

Establish consistent backswing rhythm = consistent tempo ratio

  • Use metronome app at 120 BPM as starting point
  • Pattern: 1-beat (start) → 3-beats (top) → 1-beat (impact) = 3:1 ratio
  • Hit 10 balls, target 9/10 swings within 3.0–3.5 ratio
  • Progress: Once consistent at 120 BPM, try 115 BPM
  • Expected: Std deviation ±0.73 → ±0.45 (38% improvement)

Drill #2: Transition Trigger Consistency

Verbal cue: "Back-2-3, Down-1-2" (synced to metronome)

  • Address at 60% target distance (not full swings yet)
  • Complete backswing by beat 3, pause 0.1 sec at top
  • Initiate downswing at secondary beat 1
  • Expected: Sequencing errors 29% → 12%

Week 3–4: Early Extension Awareness

Drill #3: Pelvis Lift Feedback Loop PRIORITY #2

  • Use mirror (face-on) or video — monitor belt buckle position at impact
  • Cue: "Hold your posture" / "Stay tall through the ball"
  • Start 50% backswing, progress to full swings
  • Head should be slightly behind ball at impact
  • Expected: Pelvis Lift ±1.2" → ±0.7" (42% improvement)

Drill #4: The "Wall Drill" (Anti-Early Extension)

  • Stand with back to wall, heels ~6" from wall
  • Downswing WITHOUT glutes/pelvis touching wall
  • Correct: Pelvis rotates/slides laterally without lurching forward
  • Do 2–3x per week (5 min, 15 swings each)
Phase 1 Success = 30% overall improvement in consistency + 5–8 yard distance gain

Week 5–6: Wrist Release & Lag Maintenance

Drill #5: Lag Trainer Progression PRIORITY #3

  • At top: Wrist ~90° flexion (neutral)
  • Halfway down: Wrist ~85° (lag maintained)
  • 6" before impact: Wrist releases to ~60°
  • At impact: Wrist ~45° (hands ahead of ball)
  • Cue: "Wrists stay quiet halfway down"
  • Expected: Release timing ±18% → ±8% (55% improvement)

Drill #6: Sequencing Chain Drill (Pelvis → Chest → Arm → Club)

  • Step 1 — Pelvis only (30% power, 5 balls): Feel pelvis lead before arms/chest
  • Step 2 — Pelvis + Chest (50% power, 5 balls): Lower body leading
  • Step 3 — Full sequence (75% power, 10 balls): Each link accelerates in order
  • Step 4 — Full swing (100%, 10 balls): Video verify 1-2-3-4 order
  • Expected: Sequence accuracy 71% → 88%

Week 7–8: Fitness — Posterior Chain Activation

Glute/hamstring strength, hip extension control. Maintain upright torso — no early extension pattern.

Copenhagen Squeezes (2x12)

Inner thigh/adductor strength. Stabilizes pelvis during rotation, prevents swaying.

Glute Bridge Holds (2x15)

Squeeze glutes at top, 2 sec hold. Progress to single-leg (week 7+). Trains hip extension for downswing launch.

Band Pull-Through (2x10)

Hip hinge pattern, posterior chain integration. Replicates downswing hip extension pattern.
